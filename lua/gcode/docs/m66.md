# M66

Wait on input.

**Summary**
Pauses until digital or analog input meets condition.

**Format**
`M66 P<port> L<mode> E<timeout>`

**Modes:**
- L0: Wait for low
- L1: Wait for high
- L2: Wait for pulse low
- L3: Wait for pulse high

**Example**
`M66 P1 L1 E5 (wait for input 1 high, 5 sec timeout)`
